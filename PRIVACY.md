# Privacy Policy for Local Search

**Last updated: January 5, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Local Search** Chrome extension ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection
We respect your privacy. **Local Search does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal user data.**

- **No Analytics:** We do not track your usage of the extension.
- **No Personal Information:** We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information.
- **No Search History:** We do not store or view your search queries. Your searches are processed entirely locally on your device and sent directly to Google.

## 2. Permissions
The extension requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of its functionality:

- **`tabs`**: This permission is used exclusively to open a new tab with your generated Google Search URL. We do not read or modify the content of your tabs.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This extension interacts with **Google Search** by opening search URLs. By using this extension, you are subject to Google's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service when viewing their search results pages. We are not affiliated with Google.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/rollounden/Local-Search).
